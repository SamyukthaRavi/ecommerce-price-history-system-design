# Problem Definition

## Problem Statement

During major sale events on e-commerce platforms, products often display significant discounts.

However, users cannot easily verify whether these discounts represent a genuinely good deal because there is no visibility into historical pricing.

As a result, users rely on third-party tools to check price trends before making purchase decisions.

---

## Who Experiences This Problem

- Online shoppers
- Budget-conscious buyers
- Deal hunters
- Frequent sale-event users

---

## Why This Problem Matters

- Reduces trust in the platform
- Causes decision delays
- Forces users to leave the platform
- Creates fragmented user experience

---

## Current Limitations

- No native price history feature
- No price transparency
- Dependence on external tools
